# Feature Inventory & Roadmap

## Existing Features (Detected)

### Core Calculators
- **Standard/Scientific**: High-precision math engine supporting trig, logs, powers, and combinations.
- **History**: Local storage of previous calculations.
- **Themes**: M3 Dynamic color support and custom theme gallery.

### Specialized Tools
- **Math Scanner**: Camera-based OCR and Gemini AI solver.
- **Age Calculator**: Precise age calculations between dates.
- **Unit Converter**: Extensive unit conversions (Length, Mass, etc.).
- **Currency Converter**: Offline/Online exchange rates.
- **Health (BMI)**: Body Mass Index and health metric tracking.
- **Finance (EMI)**: Loan and interest calculation.
- **Percentage & CGPA**: Academic and percentage utilities.
